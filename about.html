Expense Tracker

About Expense Tracker

Expense Tracker is a web application that helps you track and manage your expenses. It allows you to add, edit, and delete expenses, as well as categorize them for easier organization. Expense Tracker also provides a summary of your expenses, allowing you to quickly see your total spending and spending trends.

Features

  • Add, edit, and delete expenses
  • Categorize expenses for easier organization
  • View a summary of your expenses, including total spending and spending trends
  • Responsive design for optimal viewing on all devices

"Track your spend, make amends, a budget trend that never ends."
